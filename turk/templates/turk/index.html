The objective of this HIT is to command a bot to create an applet to automate some task. You will chat with the bot to describe the task. You should chat with the bot as if it were a person. The bot assumes commands to be:

  • in a single sentence,
  • fairly short, and
  • grammatical.
To give you an idea of the HIT, you will now be presented an example task, and you'll walk through a sample conversation with such a bot.
